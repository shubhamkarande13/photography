About Me

I am Shubham Karande. I am based in Kitchener, Ontario, Canada. I hail from the city of Mumbai, India.

As an amateur photographer, I have a passion for capturing beautiful moments through the lens of my camera. I am constantly learning and experimenting with new techniques to improve my skills and create stunning images that tell a story. Photography is not just a hobby for me, it's a way to express myself creatively and share my unique perspective with others. Whether I'm photographing landscapes, people, or abstract subjects, I always strive to capture the essence of my subject and convey its beauty to my audience. Through my photography, I aim to inspire others, evoke emotions, and leave a lasting impact on those who view my work.

All the images and videos are my own.
